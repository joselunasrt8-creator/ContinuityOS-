## Three Core Realizations

### Capability is not permission.

AI output alone should not trigger reality-changing execution.

### Governance must exist before execution.

Legitimacy must be validated before deploys, agents, mutations, or automation can act.

### Proof makes execution auditable.

Reality-changing actions require deterministic lineage, replay safety, and proof persistence.
